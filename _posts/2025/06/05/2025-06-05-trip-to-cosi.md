Recently, Rachel mentioned that she wanted to see the [Unofficial Galaxies](https://cosi.org/unofficial-galaxies/) exhibit at [COSI](https://cosi.org/). Since I still have challenges with taking a break and use PTO, I said let's spend a few days there.

We drove down to Columbus on Sunday. Since we picked a hotel right across the street from COSI, we parked the car for a few days. The hotel also conveniently included two complementary tickets to COSI. On Monday, we walked to COSI, got tickets to the exhibit and started exploring.
